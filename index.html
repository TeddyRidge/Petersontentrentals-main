About Us

Peterson Tent Rentals is a full service event management and supply company that was founded in 2003 through a fusion of passion for business and events.

We are dealers in: Clean Tents(50 seaters, 100 seaters, cheese tents, 1000 seater A-Frame), Chairs(plastic, chiavari, banquet, foldables), tables(round & rectangular), crockery & cutlery, decoration linen, flower stands, wedding archs, P.A system, Mobile toilets, Outside catering, Red carpets, Bouncing Castles etc.

We not only organize elegant events, we organize mesmerizing moments for you, worth a lifetime of reminiscence. Whether you are looking for collective information about managing an event, we are eagerly waiting to lighten your event with our innovation and 20yr experience in the events industry. We offer complete event planning and supply solutions for an array of events, ranging from weddings to corporate events, product launches, kids festivals, ruracio/parties, outdoor events, funerals etc.

In who's hands are you safe? That's right, Peterson Tent Rentals!
